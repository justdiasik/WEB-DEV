Recipe #2

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Beef 600g
Potato 200g
Bulgarian pepper 50 g
Carrot 50g
Radish 50g
Onion 50g
Garlic 2-3
Tomato paste 100g
Meat broth or water 1 litr

Description : How to cook lagman at home: To prepare the noodles, the flour is sifted into a bowl. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #3 Eggs and water are added to the flour. Knead the unleavened dough. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #4 Adding flour if necessary, the dough is thinly rolled into a layer about 2 mm thick. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #5 The rolled dough is sprinkled with flour, rolled into a roll and cut into thin long noodles. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #6 Boil 2 liters of water, add salt to the water (about 1 teaspoon). In boiling water, lower the noodles, bring to a boil and cook the noodles in salted water until ready for 3-4 minutes. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #7 The finished noodles are thrown into a colander, washed in cold water. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #8 To prepare a meat sauce for lagman (vaju), the meat is cut into small cubes, salted. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #9 Onions and carrots are cleaned and washed. Carrots are cut into strips, and onions are diced. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #10 Sweet peppers and radishes (optional) are cleaned, washed and cut into strip Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #11 Potatoes are peeled, washed and cut into cubes. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #12 Garlic is peeled and finely chopped. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #13 Heat a frying pan( or cauldron), add vegetable oil (or fat) to it. Spread the meat. Photo of recipepreparation: Lagman-step #14 The meat is fried over medium heat, stirring, until golden brown (10-15 minutes). Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #15 Then add carrots, onions, and sweet peppers to the meat. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #16 Mix everything and continue to fry the meat and vegetables for another 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #17 Put everything in the cauldron. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #18 Then add potatoes, cut into cubes, finely chopped garlic, tomato paste or chopped fresh tomatoes. Photo of the recipe preparation: Lagman-step #19 Season with black and red ground pepper, salt. Pour water or broth and simmer vaju (meat sauce with vegetables) on low heat until tender, 30-40 minutes. Photo of recipe preparation: Lagman-step #20 To serve the lagman, the noodles are heated by dropping them into hot water and placed in a casserole or deep plate. Pour the cooked noodles and meat sauce and sprinkle the dish with chopped herbs.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
meat 0.5kg
chicken 0.5kg
ripe tomatoes 4-5
medium-sized cucumbers 3-4
white cabbage 250g
a few green lettuce leaves
salt to taste
ground pepper
sheets of thin pita bread 5

Description : Cut the meat into thin strips of medium width, season with salt and pepper. Leave for 15-20 minutes. Finely chop the garlic. You can pass it through the press. Chop the herbs selected for the sauce with a sharp knife. Separately mix sour cream with mayonnaise. Add the garlic, herbs, and mix. Season with spices. Cut the meat soaked in salt and pepper into small pieces. Fry in vegetable oil. Cut the tomatoes into small slices. Cucumbers are peeled, cut into cubes. Finely chop the cabbage. Chop the parsley. Smear the pita bread with the sauce, lay the lettuce leaves. in any order, put the prepared meat and vegetable ingredients. Carefully fold the tortilla into an envelope. You can lightly fry it in a frying pan without oil (1 minute on each side). The temperature should be above the average.
